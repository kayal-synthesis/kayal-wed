Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how KAYAL SoulPath Institute uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function properly, remember your preferences, and provide analytics data to improve our services.

Cookies We Use

Essential cookies — Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for our services to work, including form submissions and navigation.

Analytics cookies — We use anonymised analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This data helps us improve content and user experience. No personally identifiable data is collected through analytics cookies.

Live chat cookies — Our Tawk.to live chat feature uses cookies to maintain your session during a chat conversation.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. For more information on managing cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.
