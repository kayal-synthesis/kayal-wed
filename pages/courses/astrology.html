About This Programme

This programme requires no prior knowledge of celestial mapping or sky reading. Beginning with first principles and moving systematically through to advanced timing interpretation and chart synthesis, it is designed to take anyone from zero to professional-level competency in 14 structured weeks.

Astrology is the most complex and the most time-sensitive of the four pillars. The personal sky map describes who you are with extraordinary depth — your psychology, your relationship patterns, your professional strengths, and the recurring themes of your life. Timing cycles and progressions tell you when — the exact timing windows when specific energies are activated.

The KAYAL astrology programme teaches Western celestial mapping with a strong emphasis on practical interpretation and cross-validation with the numerological pillar. By programme completion, students produce complete sky map readings with timing forecasting.
