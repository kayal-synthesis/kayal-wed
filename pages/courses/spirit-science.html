About This Programme

While we recommend completing at least one pillar programme before enrolling, this programme is open to serious beginners with genuine commitment. The curriculum is designed to take you from first encounter with consciousness studies, elemental science, and the Kabbalistic framework all the way through to full ritual integration and practitioner-level competency.

Spirit Science is not a separate discipline so much as the framework that makes the entire four-pillar system coherent. Why does every major wisdom tradition — Vedic, Hermetic, Kabbalistic, Taoist, indigenous — arrive at the same model of the human being despite having no contact with each other? What underlying reality are they all reading? This programme answers those questions directly.

Over 12 weeks, students move through a rigorous, structured curriculum spanning meditation and inner stillness, the five elements and elemental beings, the celestial hierarchy of angels, the Kabbalistic Tree of Life, the Solomonic tradition of seals and sacred symbols, sound and vibrational science, and the ethics of spiritual service. Each module is practically grounded — you will not only understand the system but work with it directly.
