About This Programme

This programme is designed for complete beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Whether you have never encountered this discipline before or you are deepening an existing practice, the curriculum takes you from first principles to professional reading competency in a single structured journey — no gaps, no assumed knowledge.

Numerology is the most accessible of the four pillars and the most immediately verifiable. Its calculations are precise — your birth date and full name produce an exact numerical signature that maps the key structures of your life: your natural gifts, your recurring challenges, the cycles your life moves through, and the four major chapters (Pinnacles) that define your decades.

This programme gives you complete fluency in the Pythagorean system as taught at KAYAL — including advanced charting techniques, Karmic Debt and Master Number interpretation, and the methodology for producing a professional written numerology report.
