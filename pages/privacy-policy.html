Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

KAYAL SoulPath Institute ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us — including your name, email address, date of birth, birth location, and any additional information you share during consultations or programme enrolments. We also collect technical data such as IP addresses and browser type through standard web analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver our services (courses, consultations, Kayal LifeOS integrations), to send programme and service updates you have opted into, to improve our website and services, and to comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All form submissions are transmitted via encrypted HTTPS connections. Consultation recordings are stored securely and deleted from our systems after 12 months unless you request otherwise.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Formspree for form processing, Tawk.to for live chat, and standard analytics tools. Each operates under their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal data with any third party beyond what is necessary to deliver our services.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@kayalsoulpath.com. We will respond within 14 business days.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact KAYAL SoulPath Institute at contact@kayalsoulpath.com.
